A message from the devs


One year ago, right in the middle of the pandemics, we were trying to figure out new ways to meet people (ya... I know... what were we thinking). So we asked ourselves: what does everybody like? PRESENTS! And that's exactly what we did: we created an app that helps you both meet people and receive presents. But, just as in real life, it's not that easy to get to know somebody. So the person you'll communicate with will be anonymous for the entire event. That way you will have to get to know the person so you can give them a cool present.
'But wait' - I hear you say - 'why would I need to give out cool presents'. Well... gift and you shall be gifted. At the end of the event there will be scoring of everyone's performances and the best one will be awarded. So what are you waiting for? Go and sign up to meet and receive.
